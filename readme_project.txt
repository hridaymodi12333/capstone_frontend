it has the following structure
Components:
    Card.js: it is the card structure of each product we have
    CategoriesBar.js: it is the categories bar under the navbar
    Gallery.js: it contains the group of card and is defined for different category of the products
    Home.js: it is the homepage
    Navbar.js: it is the navbar 
    Login.js: it is the login page
    Registration.js: it is the Registration form
